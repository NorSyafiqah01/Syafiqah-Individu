Ambition

Ambition is a motivation or a strong urge to achieve something and success. Since young, everyone encourages me to have a great ambition to live a well-lifestyle and for a bright future. Some of my ambitions are to become a librarian, kindergarten teacher and person in charge for customer service.

I want to be a librarian because librarian job scope mostly gives service for users regarding information sharing as users seek advice about any information from librarian. For example, when users go to the library to find reading materials. Besides, librarian is a glorious job because librarian deals with knowledge and provide information to another people to let them gain new knowledge about something such as about library collections and services.

Furthermore, I also like to become kindergarten teacher because I like to take care of children and play with them. In addition, I like to teach children with an interesting quiz or fun game to make them enjoy the learning process. I want the children to turn into someone who is useful, diligent, creative and intelligent when they become adult later, so I feel happy if I have the chance to teach children as the kindergarten teacher is one of my ambition too.

Another ambition of mine is I want to work as a customer service management. It is because I like to make people to feel satisfy with my service regarding tasks or inquiries. Besides, I feel responsible to do my work properly to serve the customers with the best service because it can increase their satisfaction when customer service management shows a high quality of job.
